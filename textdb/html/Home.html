Long River Tai Chi Circle

Long River Tai Chi Circle is the school of Wolfe Lowenthal, student of Cheng Man-ch'ing and author of the acclaimed:

  • "There are no Secrets"
  • "Gateway to the Miraculous"
  • "Like a Long River: Some Tai Chi Thoughts"

We practice the Professor's 'simplified' 37 posture Yang-style form. The three pillars of our study are Form, Sensing Hands, and Sword.
